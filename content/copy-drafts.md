## Tone Notes

- **Don't:** "I have had the opportunity to...", "I am passionate about...", "stakeholder alignment"
- **Do:** "I built...", "The hard part was...", "What I learned..."
- First person, present tense when possible
- Specific > vague
- Honest about gaps and limitations
- Let personality show (languages, Chile, whatever makes you *you*)
